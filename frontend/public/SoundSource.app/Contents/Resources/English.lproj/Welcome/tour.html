Get to Know SoundSource

Control your Mac's audio devices right from the menu bar. Change Output, Input, and Sound Effects settings in seconds. You may never need to open the Sound System Preference again.

Fast Adjustment of System Audio Devices

SoundSource also provides powerful control of any app playing audio on your Mac. Use Volume controls to set one application louder than others, or mute bothersome apps entirely.
 
With output controls, you can send app audio to distinct outputs. Play music through your best speakers, and everything else to a secondary device.

Powerful Per-Application Audio Control

Powerful audio effects can now be applied anywhere on your Mac. The built-in Magic Boost, Volume Overdrive, and 10-band Lagutin equalizer easily enhance the sound from your speakers. With support for advanced Audio Units, audiophiles can do even more.

Easy Audio Effects, Anywhere

For instant access to all of SoundSource's power, find it right in your menu bar.
